Put ice and all of the ingredients into a shaker

  • 2 parts bourbon
  • 1 part lemon juice
  • ½ part sugar syrup
  • ½ part egg white

Shake hard for 20 seconds to chill the liquid really well

Strain the mix into a glass filled with ice

Garnish with cherry and orange slice
